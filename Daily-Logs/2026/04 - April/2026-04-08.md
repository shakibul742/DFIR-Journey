### Wireshark Filters Used in Lab
- tcp.flags.syn==1 && tcp.flags.ack==0
- tcp.flags.syn==1 && tcp.flags.ack==1
- tcp.flags.syn==0 && tcp.flags.ack==1
- tcp.flags.syn==1

### Note
- Not all SYN packets indicate normal traffic — excessive SYN requests may signal malicious activity.
- Combining multiple filters can help detect suspicious patterns more effectively.
